Our Company
Our Company
Happy-Co started out in 1995 as a Family owned business operating from a farm and a cold store in Nubarega City, conveniently situated in dose proximity to farms in neighboring cities and major Egyptian sea ports in Alexandria Governorate. In 2010 we ex-panded our operations to include manufacturing food products for other companies intended for local market consumption and for exporting. This required the addition of new processing halls and bigger cold stores that increased our production and stor-age capacitg. After the encouraging success in manufacturing food products for others, at the beginning of 2014, we launched our own brand, YARrIA', which includes a variety of pickled vegetables along with frozen fruits and vegetables.
  • Our vision
    Our vision
